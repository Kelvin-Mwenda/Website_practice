About Us

Know About Dairy Harbor

Dairy Harbor is a comprehensive dairy farm management app designed to streamline dairy farm operations and maximize productivity. With innovative features that cover every aspect of dairy farming, Dairy Delight empowers you to efficiently manage your herd, optimize breeding, and monitor production and sales

Comprehensive Data Capture
Record essential details such as serial number, name, date of birth, and parent information for each of your cattle.
Herd Visualization
Visualize your herd with photos and other identifying details, providing a comprehensive overview of your cattle at a glance.

Why Me!

Reasons to have Harbor App!

Our goal is to develop a dairy farm management app that offers a user-friendly interface and robust functionality, enabling farmers to efficiently oversee their operations, enhance productivity, and optimize profitability through streamlined livestock management, accurate production tracking, and sales monitoring.

Our Services

Services That We Offer For Farmer

Artificial Insemination Management

Dairy Harbors' Artificial Insemination Management module helps you optimize your breeding efforts, ensuring efficient and successful artificial insemination procedures.

Milk Distribution and Sales

Dairy Harbor' Milk Distribution and Sales module streamlines the management of your milk supply chain, ensuring efficient distribution and minimizing losses.

Periodic Procedures Management

Dairy Harbors' Periodic Procedures Management module helps you stay on top of essential health maintenance practices, ensuring the optimal well-being of your cattle.

Cattle Registration Module

The Cattle Registration Module in Dairy Harbor streamlines the process of managing your herd, allowing you to efficiently organize and update cattle information.

Health Procedures Tracking

Dairy Delights' comprehensive Health Procedures Tracking module ensures the well-being of your cattle by meticulously recording all essential health-related activities.

Production Monitoring
